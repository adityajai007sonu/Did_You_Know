    The one about men and women being genetically more similar to monkeys, than to each other is terribly wrong.
    Chimps (our closest related ancestor and the one we’re most genetically alike) have 2 chromosomes more than humans. Humans and chimps are 94% alike genetically, while humans are ~99% alike genetically, between genders because of differences in the Y/X gender chromosome. So any human, no matter the gender, is always 6% different from chimps, while being ~1% different between genders.
